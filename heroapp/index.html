What is Hero?

Salesforce is a great CRM, but for users who have to update data on Salesforce regularly, it can be extremely time consuming and frustrating to use. That’s why we built Hero.app - a CRM interaction layer that’s meant to help users stay productive and save time. We designed each feature with the end users in mind, which means speed, efficiency, and usability. Hero.app combines custom sheets, forms, hotkeys, a command line interface, and more into one clean and versatile workspace to eliminate the pain points of using CRMs.

What makes Hero different?

Say goodbye to Salesforce’s clunky UI and hello to one of the best UIs modern software has to offer. Hero.app’s custom sheets allow users to apply filters, bulk update records, easily search for records, and make inline updates all in one place.

No more extra tabs for creating and updating records. Organize your ideas by keeping all your forms in one place. Set default fields for records and create accounts, contacts, leads, opportunities, notes and tasks in real time.

Mouse slowing you down? Get your gamification on and learn our myriad of keyboard shortcuts to find and update what you need in a fraction of the time.

Our command line interface was built for speed and versatility. Easily navigate through all your data and manage all your records in one centralized location.

Fully customizable experience from top to bottom. Choose the metrics you want displayed on your home page. Customize your sheets to your heart’s content. Pick your own color theme. Share and copy sheets and forms with colleagues. More customizable features coming soon!
